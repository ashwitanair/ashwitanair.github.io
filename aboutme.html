WHO AM I?

Hi! I am Ashwita Nair, a junior at Cupertino High School, and welcome to my blog! A little bit about me is that I am creative, and absolutely in love with art and designing. I am also extremely passionate about dancing. I have experience in hip hop, Bollywood, contemporary, classical, and jazz, and have been dancing for the past 11 years. I have also been playing piano (currently on level 9), and doing karate (currently a brown belt) for the past 9 years. In school, I am part of National Honor Society and California Scholarship Federation, as I love volunteering and helping others. I have also been invested being in the yearbook team for the past two years, where I originally started off as a member of the photo team, then transitioning to design editor, and currently assistant editor-in-chief. Some other hobbies that I enjoy are: listening to music, swimming, photography, baking, and traveling. Three words I would use to describe myself are creative, positive, and dedicated to everything I do!
